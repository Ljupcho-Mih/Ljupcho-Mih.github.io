Empowering Central European Business Expansion

Helping companies export, sell, find partners, and grow across borders.

Trade Missions

Our trade missions connect your company with buyers, distributors, suppliers, and public institutions across Central Europe. We organize targeted B2B agendas, site visits, and tailored business introductions to accelerate your market entry.

Partner Meetings

We arrange strategic partner meetings that help companies identify local distributors, manufacturers, investors, and technology partners. Each agenda is curated to match your exact business goals and sector needs.

Country Visits

Our country visits offer hands-on market insight, direct business introductions, and access to industry stakeholders. We work with local chambers, associations, and authorities to ensure high-impact engagement.

Market Research

We provide deep regional intelligence: market size, competitor mapping, regulatory overviews, pricing analysis, and investment incentives. Our research supports data-driven decisions across all CEE markets.

Business English

We offer professional communication training tailored for executives working internationally—presentation skills, negotiation language, email clarity, and sector-specific terminology.
